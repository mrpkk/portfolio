Vedic Timekeeper

Premium Android clock app with Vedic astrological integration

🚧 In Development

Features

🕉 Panchanga

Daily Vedic calendar: tithi, nakshatra, yoga, karana, sunrise/sunset, moon phase.

⏰ Muhurta Engine

Auspicious time selection based on Bṛhat Saṃhitā. 17 favorable + 13 unfavorable classifications.

🐾 Nakshatra Yoni

Daily totem animal based on lunar nakshatra. Vedic wisdom from Bṛhat Parāśara.

🎤 Voice Control

Hands-free operation — "What's today's muhurta?", "Set alarm for next Brahma muhurta."
